Cyclistic Bike-Share Exploratory Data analysis

An exploratory data analysis over a 12 month historic dataset to evaluate how annual subscribers and casual riders differ especially in their usage of the Cyclistic bike program and using the trends to develop a robust marketing strategy. I cleaned, visualized and analyzed the data and was able to provide insights such as the number of rides, the average trip duration and longest ride duration by customer type, the bike type preference by customers, and the most popular routes and stations. I also gave recommendations on how to optimise the marketing strategies targeted at existing casual members with the aim to convert them to annual subscribers.

Mass Shootings in the USA

This is an exploratory data analysis on the mass shootings recorded in the USA. The data is prepared for analytics, insights are extracted from it using visualisation tools and trends identified in mass shootings which includes the number of shootings over the years, the deaths and injuries caused, demographics of the shooters. This analysis and visuals can act as persuasion tools to elected government officials to enact policies that can help reduce the impact of gun violence in the society.
